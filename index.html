I am a graduate student in the Department of English at the University of Vermont.

My work is inspired by the advent of 'form' and its great historical adventure across the landscape of thought.

My research focuses on Hegel's Science of Logic and works at the intersection of literary theory and visual studies. Most recently, I have tried to develop this work in relation to literary theories of representation, modern philosophy, and the gothic. Additionally, I have an acute interest in the history of structuralism and post-rationalism.
