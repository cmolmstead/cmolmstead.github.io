I am a visiting researcher at the Institute of Philosophy-Slovenian Academy of Sciences & Arts and the University of Ljubljana; my research is funded by the U.S. Fulbright program.

My current project focuses on Hegel's Science of Logic and works at the intersection of philosophy of science, language, and ontology. I am especially concerned with the construction of models and grounding of logical systems across and in history, literature, and social theory.

I am also interested in the bringing an aesthetic dynamic to my work. Visit my multimedia philosophy project: Emptysubject
